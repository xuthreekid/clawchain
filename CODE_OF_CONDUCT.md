# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical discussion and collaboration
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take appropriate corrective actions.
